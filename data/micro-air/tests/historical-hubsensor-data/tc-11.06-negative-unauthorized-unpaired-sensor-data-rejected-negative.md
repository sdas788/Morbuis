## Steps
Negative – Unauthorized / Unpaired Sensor Data Rejected

## Expected Result
Attempt to send readings from a sensor UID that is not authorized for the user/hub (unpaired, wrong account, or invalid ownership). Verify backend rejects or quarantines the data per security rules and does not attach/store it under the user’s history. Errors are logged appropriately without exposing sensitive details.
