## Description

**Observed**

* My price options on the paywall change as I flip between Monthly and Annual

    * When Monthly is selected
    
        * Monthly: 9.99/mo
        * Yearly: $60, 5/mo
        
    * When Yearly is selected
    
        * Monthly: $6.00/mo
        * Yearly: $69.99 / $5.83/mo
        
    

**Expected**

Prices should be consistent in the UI to reflect what the user will be paying.
