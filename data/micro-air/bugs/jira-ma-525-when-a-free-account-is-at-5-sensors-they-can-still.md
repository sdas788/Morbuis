## Description

**Observed**

* Unpaid account - I do get the paywall when I click to add a sensor at the bottom, but I skip the paywall if going into the add sensor flow via the menu
* I can also add more sensors if I click “Add Sensor” directly at the end of the add Sensor flow

**Expected**

* If I am on a free account, I should always get the paywall when adding a sensor
