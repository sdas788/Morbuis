## Steps
Notification Preferences

## Expected Result
- Open the application and ensure you are logged in and have been shown an “Allow Notifications” popup from the operating system. Accept this permission before continuing.
- Once this is done, navigate to the place in which in-app notifications may appear, take note of how many notification are seen here.
- If on iOS, check to ensure Background App Refresh is set to on for the application, and that the system’s Power Saving mode is off for the duration of testing.
