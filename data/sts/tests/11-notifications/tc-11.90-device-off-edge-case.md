## Steps
Device Off

## Expected Result
- When the test device is powered down, send a notification through the backend service layer
- If the user’s login session has not expired, they should see a delayed notification from the application. This will be either a banner in the iOS notification shade, or an icon and banner in the Android status bar.
- If the user’s login session expired, they will not see a notification banner on iOS or icon on Android
- When opening the application, regardless of login status, the user will see their in-app notification if applicable
