## Outcome

The existing AppMap agent (E-006) describes what the app does. This epic adds the decision layer beneath it: a prioritized list of flows recommended for automation, each with priority (P0/P1/P2), rationale, coverage status, and a one-click "Generate YAML" handoff. Makes AppMap actionable instead of descriptive.

## Success Metric

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Clicks from AppMap to generating a new flow YAML | many (manual interpretation) | 1 |
| % of newly automated flows that originated from AppMap recommendations | 0% (pre-E-018) | >50% |

The QA Lead (especially on new RF projects where automation targets aren't obvious)
