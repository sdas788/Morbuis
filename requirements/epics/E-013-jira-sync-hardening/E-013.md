## Outcome

QA leads can trust that Jira ↔ Morbius stays consistent without manual reconciliation. Broken sync — the current state — is the hard blocker for Core Four #2 (Ticket→Repro). This epic fixes the sync's root cause, exposes a Settings health panel so drift is visible, and adds write-back + a replay queue for resilience.

## Success Metric

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Bug-sync round-trip success rate | Broken (user-reported) | >99% over 7 days |
| Sync failures visible in dashboard | 0% (silent) | 100% surfaced in health panel |
| Time-to-discover a sync failure | Days (user notices manually) | <5 min (panel alert) |
