## Outcome

Implements Core Four #1 — the highest-leverage item. When a Maestro flow fails on a stale selector, Morbius snapshots the view hierarchy, has Claude propose a replacement, validates it via re-run, and presents a reviewable proposal. Human approves → YAML updates. Target is 70% auto-resolved failures by end of Phase 1 (per brief.md target).
