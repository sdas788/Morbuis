## Status

**Parked — Bet A territory (multi-framework breadth).** Not on the active 7-phase plan. Maestro stays the single supported framework under Bet C. See [direction-2026-04.md](../../wiki/direction-2026-04.md). Do not break into stories until Bet A activates.

## Outcome

Development teams will be able to run Appium and Detox test suites alongside Maestro flows from the same Morbius dashboard — with a unified results view — eliminating the need to maintain separate tooling for teams that use different test frameworks.
