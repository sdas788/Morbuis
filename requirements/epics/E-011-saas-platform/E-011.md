## Outcome

External teams will be able to sign up for Morbius as a hosted service, collaborate with role-based access, and run tests in the cloud — without any local installation — enabling Morbius to operate as a commercial product with recurring revenue.

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Paying teams on hosted platform | 0 | 20 teams within 6 months of Phase 7 launch |
| Time from sign-up to first test run | N/A (local only) | Under 30 minutes |
| Data stored in PostgreSQL (vs. markdown files) | 0% | 100% of test, bug, and run data |
