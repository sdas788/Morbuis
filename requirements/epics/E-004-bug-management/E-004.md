## Outcome

Any test failure automatically becomes a tracked bug ticket — complete with screenshot, device info, and selector analysis — and the team can triage, investigate, and sync with Jira without leaving the dashboard.

## Success Metric

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Maestro failure → bug ticket (automated) | Manual Jira creation | 100% of ingested failures auto-create a bug |
| Jira sync coverage | 0% | All local bugs syncable bidirectionally with Jira |
