## Outcome

QA leads and PMs can see at a glance which tests are flaky, which devices have coverage gaps, and which areas of the app have no automation — without running any queries or writing scripts.

## Success Metric

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Flaky tests surfaced automatically | 0 (manual tracking) | All tests with flakiness score ≥0.4 flagged on dashboard |
| Coverage gaps visible to PM without QA | Not visible | Gaps tab shows stale, missing, and under-tested areas |
