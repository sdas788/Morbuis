## Outcome

Implements Core Four #2 (Ticket→Repro). When a bug changes state in Jira, an AI-generated Impact analysis appears in the bug modal: which tests to rerun, which to manual-verify, a regression risk score, and a reproduction narrative. Closes the "what do I do now?" gap after a fix lands.

## Success Metric

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Time from bug-fixed status → knowing what to verify | Manual triage (minutes to hours) | <3 min (auto-generated) |
| % of closed bugs with captured impact analysis | 0% | 100% |
| Agreement rate: AI-suggested related tests vs. QA-picked | — | >70% overlap (validation metric) |
