## Outcome

A QA lead can plan *which* test cases to automate and *how* without committing machine-specific Maestro paths into the repo. The Automation Plan is the authoritative card↔flow mapping: it surfaces draft flows, marks cards blocked with a reason, carries the persona/credential context a run needs, and lets a planned-but-uncommitted flow still run one-click. It turns "203 cases, which do we automate?" into a tracked, runnable plan.
